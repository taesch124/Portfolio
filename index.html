About Me

 I am a software developer for CIT Group working on database architecture and design for Treasury IT Support. I have developed interfaces to allow for Federal reporting of the assets and well as interfaces to allow customer trades executed with PNC to be captured in CIT systems. I am also currently learning Web Development through a Rutgers sponsored program.

 After graduating school in 2014 with a degree in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Southern California, I moved back home and began to learn computer programming on my own. I took a 4 month course to learn Unix, SQL, and Java and then got hired to work fot CIT. I would like to work on programming solutions to make user's lives easier while remaining simple and engaging to use.

 I am an enthusiastic video game player, which has been a passion of mine since I was a young kid playing with my brother. I also enjoy hiking and spending time on the beach when I am not in front of a computer screen.
